Introduction to Web Development (DEV109)

Instructor: Professor Brehm

Bellevue College Catalog Course - DEV109

Reflection: Before this class, I had a basic understanding of HTML and CSS. After completing this class, I have gained significant skills in JavaScript and responsive design, and I feel more confident in building dynamic web pages.

Japanese 1 (JAPN&121)

Instructor: Professor Hardt

Bellevue College Catalog Course - JAPN&121

Reflection: Japanese 1 was an exciting introduction to the Japanese language and culture. Before this class, my knowledge of Japanese was limited to a few anime phrases. Throughout the course, I learned essential grammar, vocabulary, and conversational skills. The interactive lessons and engaging activities made learning fun and effective. By the end of the class, I could hold basic conversations in Japanese and had a deeper appreciation for the culture and customs of Japan.

Business Calculus (MATH&148)

Instructor: Professor Lowry

Bellevue College Catalog Course - MATH&148

Reflection: Business Calculus was a challenging yet rewarding course that applied calculus to real-world business scenarios. I learned to use derivatives and integrals to solve problems, optimize profit, and minimize costs. The practical applications made the material engaging and relevant. By the end, I felt confident in using calculus for business analysis and decision-making.

American Government and Politics (POLS&202)

Instructor: Professor Cawthorn

Bellevue College Catalog Course - POLS&202

Reflection: Political Science was an eye-opening course that introduced me to the complexities of government and politics. I learned about different political systems, ideologies, and the impact of policy decisions on society. This course enhanced my understanding of current events and the importance of civic engagement.
